Training and Location

Training

We meet and swim every Tuesday and Thursday, between 9.00pm and 10.00pm, at Perth Leisure Pool.
The pool is 25m long and we have sole use of all 5 lanes, during our training times. Swimmers are graded in lanes based on ability and our coach, set our sessions. Occaisionally, we have use of a lane or two at the National Swimming Academy in Stirling, which is a 50m pool
